Whiteboard photo fix

Inspired by this post on HN with an ImageMagick script, this simply wraps a small webservice around the script and has a drag and drop uploader. This is not production ready in any form! Use at your own risk, though that said Flask is pretty solid. Images are stored in a temporary location and may be removed at any time so please save anything you process. It can take a minute to process (due to the convolution) so be patient! Images are scaled to 1600x pixels for resource reasons.
